About TWOCENT

Welcome to TWOCENT, a platform designed to rank FNAF fangames! The rankings provided on this website should be taken with a grain of salt. My goal is to create an enjoyable experience for users to vote on various games. I do not intend to insult or offend anyone in the process.

If you are a developer and would like your game removed from the website, please feel free to reach out to me at twocentdevelopment@gmail.com, and I'll remove your game.

Please note that every game featured on this website is owned by its respective creator, and the original GameJolt page remains the primary source of all content. Go visit and support the original creators!
